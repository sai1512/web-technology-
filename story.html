HONEST WOODCUTTER

INTRODUCTION





Once there lived an honest poor wood cutter in a village. His village was beside a jungle. He used to cut the wood from the jungle, sell in the near by market and bear his familly expenses.He


Asusually he went to the jungle.He started cutting wood near a pond Accidentally his axe fell in the pond. The pond was very deep.So he became very sad that he lost his only axe.


So he prayed to god for help. Amazingly, a strange incident took place. A fairy came out of water and asked the reson for sobbing


Then the wood cutter explained what has happened to the fairy. Then the fairy dived into the pond and brought him a golden axe and asked whether it was his axe the wood-cutter rejected it and told that it doesn't belong to him. Again the fairy dives into the pond and brings him a silver axe, the wood-cutter says no. Third time the fairy brings the wood-cutter's axe out of the pond.

The wood-cutter was very happy seeing his axe and takes it and thanks the fairy. The fairy was very pleased with his honesty and offers him all the three axes.The wood-cutter returns home with all the three axes and lives happily.









MORAL-

BE HONEST. HONESTY IS ALWAYS REWARDED
